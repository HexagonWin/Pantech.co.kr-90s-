Since its establishment in 1991, Pantech has played a leading role on the domestic information & telecommunication industry through developing state-of-the-art general telecommunication devices on its own. Pantech, established by a group of five passionate people, started business by developing wireless radio pagers.
And now it is about to leap high from the springboard as a general information & telecommunication company spreading out over the world.
